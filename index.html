About

I am an MSc student in Mechatronics Engineering at Politecnico di Torino, specializing in Software Technologies for Automation. My work sits at the intersection of reinforcement learning, imitation learning, and robot manipulation.

Currently working on RL fine-tuning of flow-matching policies for robot manipulation, building on the MSG framework (von Hartz et al., 2025) using the ReinFlow pipeline in RLBench.

Research Interests

  • RL fine-tuning of flow-matching (diffusion-based) manipulation policies
  • Intersection of control theory, imitation learning, and reinforcement learning
  • Sample efficiency and stability in policy learning
  • Sim-to-real transfer via domain randomization

Projects

RL Fine-tuning of Multi-Stream Generative Policies
work in progress · targeting publication
Applying ReinFlow (Zhang et al., 2025) to online RL fine-tune MSG (von Hartz et al., 2025), a multi-stream object-centric flow policy, in RLBench (CoppeliaSim).
